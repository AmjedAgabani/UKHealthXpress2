Need a blood sample separated? We're here to help.

  • How the process works

    • Your blood is made up of three parts, Red Blood Cells, White Blood Cells and Plasma. We use a centrifuge to seperate your blood into those 3 different components for testing.

  • Blood separation at the clinic

    • We provide daily appointments at our clinic, visit us and we can separate your blood on the day!

  • Blood separation at your home

    • For our patients who are unable to come visit us at the surgery, we have mobile phlebotomists equipped with portable centrifuge devices at hand who you can book an appointment with to collect and separate your blood from the comfort of your own home.

  • Blood separation at the workplace

    • Too busy to visit the surgery? At UK HealthXpress we understand that you may get tied up at work alot of the time, as a result we've made it possible for you to book an appointment with our mobile phlebotomists to withdraw blood from you and separate it at a time suitable to you in your work place.
